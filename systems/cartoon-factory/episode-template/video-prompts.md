# Gemini Video Prompts

## Scene Prompt Format

```text
Scene ID:
Duration:
Reference assets:
Characters:
Location:
Action:
Camera:
Emotion:
Transition in:
Transition out:
Continuity constraints:
Negative constraints:
```

## Continuity Requirements

Each prompt must include:

- approved character reference;
- exact outfit and colors;
- camera direction;
- starting pose;
- ending pose;
- transition relationship to previous scene;
- no identity changes.
