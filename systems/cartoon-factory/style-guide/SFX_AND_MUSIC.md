# SFX And Music Guide

## Sound Effects

SFX should support action and comedy timing. Do not overload every movement with sound.

Common SFX categories:

- entrance / exit;
- object impact;
- UI / notification;
- realization;
- awkward pause;
- transition whoosh;
- tiny failure;
- final punchline sting.

## Music

Music should establish rhythm but stay below dialogue.

Music brief must define:

- mood;
- tempo;
- instrumentation;
- start time;
- volume dips under dialogue;
- ending sting or fade.

## Mix Rules

- Dialogue always wins.
- SFX must not mask punchlines.
- Music should dip during important lines.
- Final beat can use a short sting if it improves the joke.
