# Agent Task: Idea Strategist

## Role
Turn trend + profile context into content hypotheses.

## Output
`content_plan.md` and `content_plan.json` with 7-14 ideas:
- title
- pillar: demo | pain-solution | case | education | founder | failure | offer
- hypothesis
- target metric
- target platform(s)
- hook
- CTA
- required asset/media

## example brand pillars
- AI-agent demos
- business pain → AI solution
- case/before-after
- education
- founder POV
- AI-agent failures/debugging
- offer/CTA posts
