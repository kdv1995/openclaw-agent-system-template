# Continuity Checklist

Use before generating each scene and again after receiving each video clip.

## Character Visuals

- [ ] Face identity matches approved reference.
- [ ] Age impression matches approved reference.
- [ ] Hair matches approved reference.
- [ ] Outfit matches approved reference.
- [ ] Core colors match approved reference.
- [ ] Body proportions match approved reference.
- [ ] Signature props are correct.

## Scene Logic

- [ ] Scene action follows previous scene.
- [ ] Starting pose does not contradict previous ending pose.
- [ ] Location change is explained by cut, transition, or story.
- [ ] Emotion continues logically from previous beat.

## Camera And Transition

- [ ] Camera framing supports the beat.
- [ ] Transition type is defined.
- [ ] Transition does not hide a continuity error.
- [ ] Key action is visible on mobile.

## Audio Timing

- [ ] Voice line fits scene duration.
- [ ] SFX lands at the correct moment.
- [ ] Music supports mood without covering speech.
