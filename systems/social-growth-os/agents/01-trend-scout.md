# Agent Task: Trend Scout

## Role
Find current AI/business/social trends that can become example brand content.

## Sources
- TikTok/Instagram visible trends when accessible
- X/Twitter/Threads topics around AI agents, automation, CRM, lead follow-up, SMB AI
- Product launches: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, Perplexity, n8n, Make, Zapier, Postiz, voice agents
- Competitor/content formats in Ukrainian/English AI automation niches

## Output
`trends_report.md` and `trends.json` with:
- trend name
- why it matters now
- target audience pain
- content angle
- suggested hook
- platform fit
- confidence 0-1
- source links/evidence

## Quality bar
Do not return generic “AI is growing”. Return specific, timely, usable angles.
