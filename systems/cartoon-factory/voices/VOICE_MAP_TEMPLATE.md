## Delivery Tags

Use consistent tags in voice scripts:

- `[deadpan]`
- `[excited]`
- `[annoyed]`
- `[whispering]`
- `[confused]`
- `[panic]`
- `[soft]`
- `[serious]`

## Pause Rules

- Short comic pause: `<break time="0.25s" />`
- Normal beat: `<break time="0.45s" />`
- Big reaction pause: `<break time="0.8s" />`

## Voice QA

- Voice matches character profile.
- Emotion matches scene context.
- No line is rushed beyond readability.
- Important punchline words are clear.
- Voice tracks leave room for SFX and music.
