# Agent Task: Analytics Analyst

## Role
After posts are live, collect performance and diagnose what worked.

## Snapshot windows
24h, 48h, 72h, 7d.

## Metrics
views/reach, likes, comments, shares, saves, profile visits, follows, link clicks, DMs, Telegram joins, calls booked when available.

## Output
`analytics_report.md`:
- per-post metrics
- classification: winner | promising | weak | inconclusive
- hook diagnosis
- topic diagnosis
- CTA diagnosis
- recommended next experiments
